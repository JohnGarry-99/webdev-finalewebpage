YouTube

Introduction

YouTube began in 2005 and was started by Chad Hurley, Steve Chen and Jawed Karmin. It began to really take off from about 2006 onwards and since then has become one of the most common apps on every mobile or tablet device it has become so popular that it is often pre-installed on these devices when they are bought from stores.

Registration

To make a YouTube account first you will need an email. Once that is set up you can then make your account and watch and post all the content you want.

How to use the App

The app is very easy to use at the navigation bar at the bottom of your screen to select the home, explore, subscription, inbox or library pages. in all of these sections you will be able to find videos to watch once you find one just click the video and there you go.
